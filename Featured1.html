Featured Cats

Fluffy

Fluffy is a special cat who has been through a lot in his life. He was abandoned by his previous owner, frightened and hungry. Despite his rough start, Fluffy has a heart full of love and affection to give. He may be a bit shy at first, but once he learns to trust you, he will be the most loyal companion you could ever ask for.

Fluffy's journey to find a loving forever home has been a challenging one, and he deserves a second chance at happiness. With your care and understanding, Fluffy can overcome his past and thrive in a warm and caring environment. He will be forever grateful to the person who gives him a safe and loving home.

Age: 3 years
Health: Vaccinated, neutered, and microchipped
Adoption fee: Waived (incentive to find him a loving home)

Whiskers

Whiskers is an endearing and charming cat known for his clumsiness. With every step he takes, he seems to find a way to make everyone around him smile. His adorable antics and playful personality will fill your home with joy and laughter.

Whiskers may occasionally knock over a vase or slip while chasing his tail, but his loving and affectionate nature will more than make up for any mishaps. He has a heart as big as his paws, and he is eager to share his love with a forever family.

Age: 2 years
Health: Vaccinated, neutered, and microchipped
Adoption fee: Waived (incentive to find him a loving home)
